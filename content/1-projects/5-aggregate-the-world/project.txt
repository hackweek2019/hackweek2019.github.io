## Introduce a technology which allows declaratively aggregates various Solutions from different Vendors in SolutionBuilder
The technology intuitively discovers endpoints exposed by 3-d Party and extracts them to SolutionBuilder, where they can be used for constructing new User Intergaces, or for building intricate business logic in Workflows

Demo case. In 2 minutes configure connectivity with different APIs (e.g. Google Calendar, VSTS), build an action which Action, with workflow

Key words: 
* REST, 
* Soap, 
* Wsdl, 
* swagger, 
* Authentication, 
* Workflow Studio, 
* Solution Builder

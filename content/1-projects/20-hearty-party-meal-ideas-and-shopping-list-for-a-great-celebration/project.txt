## HEARTY PARTY: Meal Ideas and Shopping List for a Great Celebration

Throwing a party but donâ€™t know what to serve to your guests?

HEARTY PARTY is an app created to provide help for those who plan a party and get lost in a supermarket and then end up with way too much salsa or run out of bread in the middle of the party.

A wide range of meal ideas makes it easy to create a shopping list to meet the needs of a certain party format.

HEARTY PARTY gives you a chance to choose the format (BBQ, brunch, picnic, dinner), a certain holiday (Christmas, Birthday, Baby shower), the number of people participating and even create a theme party!
